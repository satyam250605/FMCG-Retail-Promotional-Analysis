Atliq Mart's Promotional Campaign Analysis aims to determine the contributions Different Promotional Campaigns at Atliq Mart made towards Sales Volume and Revenue across Store/City/Product Category/Individual Product during the Festive Season of Diwali (2023) and Sankranti (2024). The Purpose of this analysis is to provide Actionable Insights into future Campaign Planning.

This Analysis was accomplished through SQL queries executed against MySQL, and emphasis has been placed on providing Business Reasoning and Business Insights based on the analysis, rather than just querying data from MySQL Database.

Atliq Mart has several Retail Stores in South India and offers Promotional Campaigns during the Festival Periods of the Year (Diwali and Sankranti), and not all Promotions result in the same performance within Product Categories, Store Locations, and Cities.

The Analysis has answers to various areas or dimentions like Store-wise performance, Product-Category performance analysis, and Promotional or Campaign Level Analysis.
